How It Works

What Affects Your Insurance Cost?

The model considers six key factors that collectively explain 86.4% of the variation in insurance charges across the dataset.

Age

Older individuals typically incur higher medical costs due to increased health risks

Smoking Status

The single most influential feature — smokers pay significantly more in charges

BMI

Higher BMI is associated with greater health risks and higher insurance premiums

Children

Number of dependents covered under the insurance plan

Sex

Gender plays a small but measurable role in insurance cost estimation

Region

Geographic location affects pricing across Southwest, Southeast, Northwest, Northeast
